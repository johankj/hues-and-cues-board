About Hues and Cues

Hues and Cues is a vibrant game of colorful communication where players are challenged to make connections to colors with words.

How to Use This Board:

  • Random Select: Click the "Random Select" button to randomly choose a color from the board
  • Manual Select: Click any color cell to select it manually
  • Preview: Hover over any color to see a large preview (when no color is selected)
  • Clear: Click the "Clear Selection" button to deselect the current color
  • Toggle Preview: Show or hide the color preview panel

The Game:

In the actual board game, players take turns as the "Cue Giver" who chooses a color from the board and gives one-word clues to help other players guess the correct color square. The board features a rainbow spectrum of colors for creative wordplay!
